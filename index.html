Surya Mattu
Hi! My name is Surya Mattu. I'm an artist and engineer based in Brooklyn.
This is my personal site where you can find my work and the easiest way to contact me
I am a fellow at Data&Society where I have been investigating how our wireless devices leak data and the impact that has on us.
I have also been a contributing researcher at ProPublica and have been working on   Machine Bias, a series that aims to highlight how algorithmic systems can be biased and discriminate aginst people.
